# Rebranding

**Overview:** A full brand refresh to modernize Tecit's visual identity and align packaging, web, and marketing collateral.

**Role:** Lead Designer — visual system, brand guidelines, and UI direction.

**Tech & Tools:** Figma, Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop

**Challenges:** Creating a cohesive identity that reads clearly across small product labels and large-format web headers.

**Outcome:** Delivered a scalable visual system, updated guideline documentation, and a set of assets for web and packaging that improved brand consistency across channels.
